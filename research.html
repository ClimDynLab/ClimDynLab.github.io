Research Themes

Our lab focuses on advancing understanding of monsoons, climate variability, and data-driven atmospheric science.

Monsoon Dynamics

We investigate the physical processes governing the Indian Summer Monsoon, including large-scale circulation, ocean-atmosphere interactions, and land surface feedbacks. Our work helps improve prediction and representation in climate models.

Climate Change Impacts

We assess how global warming affects the timing, intensity, and spatial patterns of monsoon rainfall. This includes studying future projections using CMIP6 models and quantifying uncertainty in regional climate outcomes.

AI/ML Applications in Climate Science

We develop and apply machine learning and deep learning models for weather forecasting, anomaly detection, and improving long-range monsoon prediction. Emphasis is placed on explainability and integration with physics-based models.

Extreme Events

The lab explores mechanisms behind extreme rainfall, heatwaves, and droughts over South Asia. We focus on their increasing frequency and intensity due to climate change and use observational datasets and high-resolution simulations.
